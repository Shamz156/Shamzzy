Narrative & Sequence ‚Äì Animatic Project

This animatic is a draft version of my final animation, created as part of the Narrative & Sequence module. I began by sketching frames by hand and then assembling them in Adobe Premiere Pro to refine the pacing, transitions, and storytelling. Creative Showcase

Capturing Time Through Growth

This piece explores the theme of time through a personal and heartfelt approach. For this project, I documented the growth of my niece by capturing a series of photographs of her hands and feet from birth to the age of two. This transformation visually represents the passage of time, highlighting the beauty of growth and change. Through this work, I aimed to create a meaningful narrative that reflects the fleeting yet precious nature of early childhood.

Interdisciplinary ‚Äì Bootlegging

For this project, we explored the use of Augmented Reality (AR) to engage with and edit physical artifacts. The goal was to select an artifact from the Bishopsgate Institute's collection and creatively interact with it using AR tools. My chosen artifact was Backstreet by Guy Burch, which is associated with the history of a gay fetish club. Inspired by the themes of this iconic piece, I decided to integrate a gay fetish symbol into the AR experience. This symbol embodies the cultural significance of the space, representing not just a physical club but also a space of expression, identity, and community within the LGBTQ+ culture. By applying AR editing techniques, I was able to transform the artifact's original context, blending the historical significance of the piece with modern interpretations of sexuality and identity.
